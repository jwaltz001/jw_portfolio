About Me

My passion for computer programming began with a series of conversations I had with a friend who is a programmer himself. With his encouragement, and after conducting personal research into the field, and spending time in personal reflection and conversations with my wife, I decided to look into a computer programming boot camp. This change in the path of my career has reinvigorated my love of logical problem solving in a new and powerful way. I find great satisfaction being able to think through problems and concepts to find solutions that are both innovative and comprehensive. My experience as a teacher allows me to understand and explain important ideas and concepts quickly and thoroughly. I create work environments where learning and growth is highly valued and encouraged and personal improvement of both products and people is continually sought.
